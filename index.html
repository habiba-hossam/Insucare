Our Story

Insucare company is a locally owned company by Dr.Mina Tharwat (founder) and Dr.Sanaa Hossam (co-founder) the company's main products are insulin pumps. Insulin pumps are devices that regulate insulin and blood sugar. They offer people with diabetes a flexible option for diabetes management.
We seek to facilitate dealing with medical devices such as insulin injections, by manufacturing a device that helps the elderly and children inject the correct doses of insulin with less pain, less risk of low and high blood glucose, fewer insulin injections, more options for meal bolus insulin, and enjoy a more flexible lifestyle.

Diabetes Types

Prediabetes
Prediabetes is a condition
characterized by blood
glucoselevels that are
higher than normal but not
yet in the range of diabetes.
It serves as a warning sign
and an opportunity for
intervention to prevent or
delay the onset of type 2
diabetes.
Type 1 Diabetes
Type 1 diabetes is a condition
in which the body's immune
system mistakenly attacks
and destroys the insulin-
producing cells in the
pancreas. As a result, the body
is unable to produce insulin.
People with type 1 diabetes
require lifelong insulin therapy
to survive.
Type 2 Diabetes
Type 2 diabetes is characterized
by high blood sugar levels,
insulin resistance, and
insufficient insulin production.
This type can be managed
through lifestyle modifications
like healthy eating, regular
exercise, and in some cases oral
medications.
Pregnancy Diabetes
Pregnancy diabetes develops
during pregnancy due to
hormonal changes. Controlling
blood sugar levels through
healthy life routine, and in
some cases insulin therapy is
essential to prevent
complications for both
the mother and her fetus.
